Brand Identity

The gap year / overseas work-experience market is saturated to say the least, there are literally hundreds of companies vying for a slice of the pie. Targeting a tricky audience of 16-25 year olds Gap Medics needed an identity that was bold and different - a breath of fresh air in a storm of A N Other brands and companies.

A striking logo supported with a custom typeface and vibrant colour palette is what makes Gap Medics shout louder than their competitors. The bold simplicity of the system means it looks as good on a fax as it does embroidered, printed, on the screen - even blown up to 8ft2 on the side of a house in Africa.

Website

As an international company, Gap Medics needed an international digital solution, so we delivered a centrally managed suite of 3 websites to cover the key markets - Europe, US & Canada, and Australasia.

The system allows the Gap Medics team to completely control content across the 3 sites independently to tailor the content and experience to suit the subtle differences from market to market.

The underlying system integrates seamlessly with all internal booking systems - but we’d hope it would, because we built that as well!

live site: gapmedics.co.uk

Customer Relationship Management

We introduced a system that provides the customer with a login area for trip management and a CRM system rolled into one. With internal activity automatically triggering external communications, building excitement over time and feeding social media, email and SMS channels.

In doing this we are supporting the marketing, sales, operations and finance functions. Providing management oversight and a system designed to lead staff through their critical tasks.

So we not only provide a consistent brand from end to end and help Gap Medics sell their services but we also provide them with tooling in support of their staff and their customers.

Printed Collateral

To strengthen brand awareness across all channels we worked with Gap Medics to develop a suite of printed collateral from stationary to brochures, to pop-up stands all the way to t-shirts.

Online Advertising

We produced a set of online banner ads to help Gap Medics get their message out. The strategy utilised Google’s re-marketing system, serving ads to users who had previously visited Gap Medics which in turn yields a higher than average conversion rate.
