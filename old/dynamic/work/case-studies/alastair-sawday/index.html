Brand Consistency

We developed a design framework to bring brand consistency across all of Canopy & Stars’ e-communications from their newsletters through to booking confirmations and reminders.

The previous solution offered a single template through which the C&S team had to hand-code each and every time they needed to send any communications. Ouch.

This template was a single column layout, bloated with extraneous images and resulted in heavyweight emails that recipients did not engage with - often missing promotions and competitions.

We introduced a suite of templates for specific tasks with a focus on flexibility, a ‘plug & play’ type solution that allows the C&S team to craft elegant and effective communications without impacting on the brand or design.

For technical delivery we looked to the awesome Campaign Monitor, which gives C&S the power to personalise, segment and target their communications and gives them an unprecedented eye on performance; something which the previous solution failed to deliver.

Results

8.5% Increase in Open Rate

6.5% Increase in Click Throughs (Improved Specificity)

75% Reduction in Administration & Management Time
