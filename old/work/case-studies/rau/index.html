Website

We developed a large scale, custom web-platform to allow the RAU to manage their content efficiently and effectively. The system allows for tiered administration, letting the RAU delegate responsibility for content whilst maintaining overall control where needed.

The structure and visual system used for organising courses was mirrored across online and the printed prospectus creating a seamless brand experience across different channels.

With universities needing to be more competitive than ever, getting students to Open Days is a key activity. We introduced clear promotion of the dates, with indicators for capacity, integrated with a simple booking form. This seemingly simple change resulted in the University needing to add open days to meet demand.

Mobile Solution

Mobile is a concern for any brand or organisation, nonetheless for the RAU. We analysed the behaviours of site users across different platforms to ensure we took the right approach.

That insight showed us that certain content and functionality needed to be more prominent so we chose to develop a specific version of the site for mobile. The mobile site features revised interfaces, layout and structure, all designed to create an intuitive, quick experience focussed on delivering content quickly.

Virtual Tour

Overseas students are a high priority for most HE institutions, the RAU included. The RAU needed to reach out to these audiences so at the heart of the site sits a virtual tour of the campus.

Intended to give visitors a real flavour of the RAU, the virtual tour is framed under ‘Living, Learning & Leisure’, the main aspects of university life and each section is punctuated by videos highlighting what makes the RAU one of the leading institutes in the world.
