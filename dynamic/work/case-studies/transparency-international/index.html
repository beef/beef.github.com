Infographics

Since 1993, Transparency International have worked tirelessly to raise awareness of international corruption and to create a coalition of interests from both the public and the private sectors to combat it.

With their secretariat in Berlin providing international leadership, TI now has over 100 hundred chapters across the globe, each working at a national level to research and publish data, raise awareness and lobby government to put an end to corruption.

Originally only publishing one yearly report, the Corruptions Perception Index, TI’s work has expanded to cover numerous, more focussed reports on particular areas of interest; politics & government, defence, education, climate change, health, forestry and more.

Over 4 years we’ve helped them to deliver different reports as interactive infographics to help build engagement and raise awareness of their work. Front of house there’s a heavy emphasis on using javascript and css3 while behind the scenes the data is parsed from simple text files to allow the TI team to easily manage the data.
