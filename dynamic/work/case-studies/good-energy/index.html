Website

We delivered a fully featured web-platform which allows Good Energy to bring their brand to life and create rich, persuasive pages.

We completely overhauled the customer journey, condensing a lengthy and confused switching process into a streamlined 3 step process that can be completed in about 5 minutes.

Under the hood, the site has all the features you’d expect; full content management, A/B splitting, full integration with internal systems, and detailed funnel tracking and analytics.

live site: goodenergy.co.uk

Results

75% Increase in Average Page Views

Comparison App

In a time of recession, when the cost of energy is higher than ever, how do you motivate customers to switch?

The proposition to switch to renewable energy can’t be driven by cost, it’s almost always a bit more pricey - so we turned the idea of price comparison into supplier comparison, letting customers see the benefits of choosing Good Energy over other suppliers without slipping into ‘greenwash’.
