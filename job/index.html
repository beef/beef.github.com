Rails Developer

Junior to middleweight
  • Competitive Salary
  • To start ASAP
  • Based in Bristol

Beef are looking for a Rails developer who is looking to gain more technical and professional experience. You will have been actively developing in Rails, Ruby and the wider eco-system for the last twelve months and been employed in a development role for a couple of years. We are “full-stack” developers. You should have experience in other development languages and platforms. This should be combined with a passion for and an understanding of the other technologies that go into producing successful digital solutions. Without going into technology name checks we do expect you to have experience in the following; Ruby, Rails, HTML/CSS, Javascript, RSpec/Cucumber, Git, Relational & NoSQL Databases.

You'll be a pragmatist with broad experience developing for the web who is looking to develop a more in depth understanding. You'll be happy to work on a variety of different tasks, supporting our clients and our Lead Application Developer. You will be able to get stuck in and use your initiative but know when to put your hand up. You will need to understand the principles of Testing (Capybara, RSpec etc), Version Control (Git is what we use) and be comfortable to code HTML, CSS and Javascript. This is as well as being able to hit the ground running supporting and developing Rails projects.

It goes without saying but... you’re going to be turning up on time, have a sense of humour, be productive and not be afraid of a bit of hard graft. In return we will give you a relaxed and pleasant place to work with a great team where you can learn more about how stuff ticks and get better at what you do.

Top line responsibilities include;

  • Client support work across different projects
  • Assisting the Lead Application Developer in ongoing development
  • Assisting the Studio Management staff with incoming enquiries and issues
  • Basic administration and housekeeping tasks
  • Implementation of on going team tools and support technologies
  • Project work and development
  • Working with and supporting the front end team

In return we’ll provide you with;

  • Cash every month
  • A pension scheme (if you want one)
  • Time off
  • A lovely team
  • A nice office
  • ...and good biscuits (and coffee)

If this all sounds like a perfect fit then drop us a line at jobs@wearebeef.co.uk with the following;

  • CV
  • Any relevant work or projects (and a bit about them)
  • Github Account

If you are an agency reading this then - seriously - please don’t contact us regarding this vacancy.
You’ll just annoy us if you do, then we won’t want to talk to you again.
