Senior Producer

  • Salary: Competitive

So here’s the thing. We’re getting up to our necks in clients & projects and it’s getting out of control. We need a Senior Producer to join the team and help us get stuff done.

Like any agency, it’s fast paced and sometimes hectic here so you’ll be super organised. We'll be counting on you to ensure everything gets done to time and budget, whilst still being an awesome piece of work. For multiple clients at the same time.

We’re looking for someones who’s a digital native - you'll not only understand the landscape, you'll be a part of it. The right candidate will have an agency background and ideally have a Prince 2 or Agile badge. If not, then be able to show you’ve delivered projects in those frameworks.

These are the top line responsibilities;

  • Day to day management of project resources
  • Be a primary point of contact for the client
  • Troubleshooting & resolution of project issues
  • Identifying scope changes and managing change requests
  • Ensure projects are delivered to time and budget
  • Assist in client development and new business pitches
