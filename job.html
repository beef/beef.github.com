Application Developer with Experience of Rails

  • Salary: Competitive
  • Start Date: ASAP
  • Location: Bristol


Beef are looking for an application developer with junior level experience in Ruby and Rails and a solid experience in PHP and Off the shelf CMS’s such as (but not limited to) Wordpress, Expression Engine or ModX. You'll be a pragmatist with broad experience developing for the web who is looking to develop a more in depth understanding. You'll be happy to work on a variety of different tasks, supporting our clients and our Lead Application Developer. You will be able to get stuck in and use your initiative but know when to put your hand up. You will need to understand the principles of Testing (capybara, rspec etc), Version Control (GIT is what we use) and be comfortable to code HTML, CSS and Javascript.

Whilst supporting our Rails and Ruby applications will form a good part of your focus you will also be working with and supporting the front end team developing in PHP and off the shelf CMS's on projects and support tasks. Experience in things like Backbone and its ilk or mobile development will make us like you even more.

It goes without saying but... you’re going to be turning up on time, have a sense of humour, be productive and not be afraid of a bit of hard graft. In return we will give you a relaxed and pleasant place to work with a great team where you can learn more about how stuff ticks and get better at what you do.

These are the top line responsibilities;
  • Client support work across different projects
  • Assisting the Lead Application Developer in ongoing development
  • Assisting the Studio Management staff with incoming enquiries and issues
  • Basic administration and housekeeping tasks
  • Implementation of on going team tools and support technologies
  • Project work and development
  • Working with and supporting the front end team
In return we'll give you;
  • Cash every month
  • A pension scheme (if you want it)
  • Time off
  • Lovely team
  • Nice office
  • Good biscuits (and Coffee)


If this all sounds like a perfect fit then drop us a line at jobs@wearebeef.co.uk with the following;

  • CV
  • Any relevant work or projects (let us know a bit about them)
  • Github Account
