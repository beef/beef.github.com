Who are we?

Beef was founded in 2004 and has quickly garnered a reputation for innovative work with both creative and technical excellence. We pride ourselves on pushing creative and technical boundaries to provide high-quality solutions to our clients. Digital thinking & doing.

What's the role?

We require a full time middle-weight to senior developer to join our small but enthusiastic team. You will have been actively developing in Rails for the last twelve months and been employed in a development role for three years. We are “full-stack” developers. You should have experience in other development languages and platforms. This should be combined with a passion for and an understanding of the other technologies that go into producing successful digital solutions.

What are we looking for?

Someone who reflects our commitment, approach and culture. Someone whose attitude, experience and capabilities reflect the seniority of the role. This means attention to detail, clear communication, strategic thinking and the ability to manage workloads and timescales.

What's it like at Beef?

We are a team of committed, multi-disciplined and passionate practitioners. We work with a variety of open source technologies spanning Ruby to PHP and Rails to Wordpress. We build everything from applications to websites and games. All of this is delivered using management processes that encourage collaboration and the efficient delivery of measurable results to our clients and their users. Members of our team make contributions to various projects and industry groups as well as maintaing an outside interest in a variety of subjects and disciplines.

We are committed to making you a happy, productive member of our team. Over the course of your employment we conduct reviews to assess your progress and to gather feedback as to how we can help you reach your goals and objectives.

As a group of folk we are a close knit and friendly bunch. We like to instil an open, good humoured culture and get together for days out, beers and one another's events and endeavours.

In short we think Beef is a great place to work. You'll love it.
