Junior Office Administrator

  • Salary: Competitive
  • Start Date: ASAP
  • Location: Bristol

The Role

Does the idea of helping run a studio so everything runs smooth like custard appeal to you?

Do you relish the idea of analyzing data and statistics and preparing jaw-dropping reports that make a difference?

Are you nimble, passionate and able to think of useful things to do without being told all the time?

If you answered yes to those questions then read on.

We’re looking for someone to come in and work with the account team to help administrate the studio - making sure stuff gets done on time and Bristol fashion. We also want you to track those things we do for our clients and make reports on performance and stuff like that.

There will be some more dull aspects to the job like making sure there’s pens in the drawer, paper in the printer and that kind of thing, but every job has a bit of nonsense that comes with it. On the upside you’ll be in charge of biscuits.

We’ll make up for all of this by paying you money, giving you a comfy chair in a nice office and generally be a pretty cool, relaxed place to work. Things do go haywire sometimes and that’s just the way it is with the kinds of things we do. But we’re hoping that you can help us make the crazy times as infrequent and manageable as possible.

On top of the money, you’ll enjoy a generous holiday allowance and the chance to learn new skills.

Also, although this is a somewhat ground-level position, we’re hoping that you can flourish into a fabulous Beeflet as the job has a future that lies in Project Management and Account Handling. When that happens you’ll lose the biscuit responsibility, but such are the woes of career progression.

This role would suit someone who’s fresh out of education and/or looking for an in-road into an agency with their sights set on a more management based career.

Seeing as you’re the sort of person who enjoys lists, here’s a list of some of the key responsibilities;

Reporting to : Account Director

  • Creating Utilization Reports from Timesheets
  • Creating Monthly Account Reports
  • Assisting Account Team with Internal Systems
  • Managing Office Supplies
  • Maintaining Overview of Customer Contracts & Renewals

    • Obvious things we need you do;

    • Answer the phone sometimes
    • Be presentable in a casual way
    • Be cool
